# Moreden Club
> Onboarding Nigerian youth onchain through culture, not crypto education.

**Location:** Nigeria (University-based)
**Focus:** Culture • Content • Onchain Ownership
**Powered by:** Zora & Base

---

## What We Are
Moreden Club is a student-led creative collective using film, visuals, storytelling, and IRL experiences to onboard young Nigerians onchain — without forcing them to "learn crypto."
We make Base the invisible infrastructure behind culture.

---

## The Problem
- Web3 onboarding is too technical for most young people
- Nigerian youth already create culture online, but not onchain
- Existing communities lack distribution and real incentives

---

## Our Solution
- Content is released on Zora
- We televise culture onchain
- IRL events and live streams turn curiosity into participation
- Members grow together as a collective, not in isolation

---

## How It Works
1. **Create** — Members release content individually and as a collective on Zora
2. **Distribute** — The club amplifies top content through socials, IRL streams, and campaigns
3. **Onboard** — New creators and viewers interact with Base naturally via Zora
4. **Retain** — Members stay for ownership, visibility, community, and upside

This loop repeats.

---

## Factions (Modular Structure)
- **Swag Archive** — documentaries on Nigerian tattoo artists, designers, skaters, directors
- **Content & Media** — short-form, film, live documentation
- **Trading & Research** — creator coins, attention markets, onchain activity
- **IRL & Events** — campus activations, onboarding sessions, live streams
